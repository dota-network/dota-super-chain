
welcome to dota2.0 network!

This is a super chain of the dota community, compatible with substrate's NPOS consensus and Ethereum EVM.


The Dota Super Chain is the central component of the Dota easter plan, marking a new beginning. Its community developers will redefine the mission of the Dota community by creating a powerful blockchain network with advanced technology that connects ecosystems such as Polkadot, Ethereum, TON, and BTC. This network aims to achieve a unified decentralized finance landscape while also building a strong social and gaming network. The community adheres to values of freedom, fairness, and openness, with the objective of establishing the largest Web3 developer DAO in the world.
